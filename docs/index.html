Bessam Ben abdellatif

Director of Photography
|video editor & filmmaker

I am a filmmaker, Director of Photography, photographer, and video editor with over seven years of experience crafting cinematic visuals through light, color, and storytelling. A graduate in audiovisual advertising from the Art School of Nabeul, Tunisia, I create films, commercials, event recaps, reels, product visuals, and portrait imagery across long-form and short-form productions. Throughout my career, I have collaborated on numerous national and private projects, managing production teams to ensure smooth workflows. My work focuses on building strong visual atmospheres through natural and artificial lighting—shaping contrast, shadows, and highlights to create depth and emotion. I pay close attention to composition and color balance to enhance storytelling, whether designing custom sets in studio or selecting impactful locations outdoors. I explore a wide range of visual styles—from modern cinematic to vintage, cyber, arthouse, and indie—adapting each project to its narrative tone. Driven by passion and creativity, my goal is to craft compelling visual experiences and tell meaningful stories.
